About Benefits Research Company

Family‑owned brokerage with decades of experience helping employers navigate benefits, compliance, and employee communications.

Since 1979, we’ve partnered with businesses to design benefits that fit their workforce and budget. We combine market benchmarking, funding strategy, and vendor management with practical compliance support so you can focus on running your business.

Whether you’re evaluating fully‑insured options, exploring level‑funded plans, or considering self‑funded with stop‑loss, we’ll help you weigh trade‑offs and manage the change with your team. And each year, we actively negotiate with carriers at renewal to keep your plan costs competitive.
